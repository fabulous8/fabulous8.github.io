Baker Black Label Turnout Sheet 74

5/A Baker Black Label Turnout Sheet is made to the same design specifications as the popular Black Label Turnout Blankets, but with insulation layer omitted, the 5/A Baker Black Label Turnout Sheet is intended for milder climates, or more moderate temperatures. This economical version features gusseted shoulders, fleece withers, bias black surcingles and trim, tail flap, stainless steel hardware and front buckles. The outer shell is a 600-denier polyester Baker plaid with an 8" wide black polyester strip along the bottom of each side of the sheet between the Baker plaid and trim. A waterproof/breathable membrane bars water penetration yet allows water vapor from perspiration to pass through to the outside.
