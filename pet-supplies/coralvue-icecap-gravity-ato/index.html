The IceCap Gravity ATO automatically replaces evaporated water in freshwater and marine aquariums without the use of electricity. To use it, screw the base to the bottle, then mount it to tank rim. When the water level drops to reveal the shorter tube, the water in the bottle automatically flows into the tank do to the pressure difference. Includes: Gravity ATO base; three mounting inserts for different rim sizes; 500 ml bottle; silicone tubing.
