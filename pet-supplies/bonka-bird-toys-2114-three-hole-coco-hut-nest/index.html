Natural coconut with three large holes and a hanging string. Great hut and chew toy. Looks great with its natural colors. 100% bird safe.

The 2114 3 Hole Coco Hut is a natural way for your small beaked buddy to rest and relax! The 2114 three hole coconut is all natural and the coconut can vary in size from approx 3.5 inches diameter to 4.5 inches diameter and the rope added to the top gives it a total height of approx 7 to 8 inches high. This toy is a natural coconut with three reasonably sized holes drilled into the sides of it. The top of 2114 has a small hole drilled with a piece of twine strung through it. This toy can be easily installed in most cages and is 100% bird safe.
