MaxLINE 1" piping by 1" male NPT straight adaptor fitting (Part number M8004). This MaxLINE fitting is specifically designed for the MaxLINE 1" tubing made by RAPIDAIR and is great for adding regulators, filters, and other equipment in the middle of the air line that need a 1" male NPT hook up.
