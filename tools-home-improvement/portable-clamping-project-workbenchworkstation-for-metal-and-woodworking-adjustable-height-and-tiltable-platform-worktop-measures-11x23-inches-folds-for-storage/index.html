The SPEEDJAW WS-023 (93335) Portable Clamping Project Station is an ideal work platform for light and medium-duty metal and woodworking. The worktable surface measures 11-5/8-inches long and 23-5/8-inches wide when fully closed yet can open to be used as a wood and metalworking clamp. The rapid adjustment levers can be used to quickly open the jaws to a maximum of 9-inches in width and the rotary clamp handles can be turned independently to provide maximum control when clamping odd shaped work pieces and projects. The work platform can be tilted up to 90-degrees with notched-in presets at 25, 50, and 80-degrees to let you get the best angle for the job at hand. 8 total bench dogs are included, 4 small single surface and 4 larger rotating multi-surface stops to help you better secure a variety of materials and shapes. Standing 28.5-inches tall at the lowest setting the table top can be raised to a maximum height of 40-inches letting you work at whatever height is most comfortable for you. When work is done, the entire workstation can be folded down for easy storage. For additional versatility, the work surface features 3/4-inch holes for use with the optional SPEEDJAW 79217 and 79218 toggle clamps. SPEEDJAW Tools are specifically designed to save time and money. With patented features that allow fast adjustability, our precision-made clamping tools and low-profile bench vises are renowned for their strength and durability.
