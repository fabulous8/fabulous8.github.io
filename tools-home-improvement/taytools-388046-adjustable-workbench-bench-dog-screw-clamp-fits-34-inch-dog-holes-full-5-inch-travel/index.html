Taytools 388046 Adjustable Workbench Bench Dog Screw Clamp, Fits 3/4 Inch Dog Holes, Full 5 Inch Travel
$16.99
Check on amazon

This adjustable in-line bench dog clamp allows you to secure work flat to your workbench anywhere there are 3/4” dog holes. The post is just shy of 3/4”, so will slip in and out of any 3/4 inch dog hole. Used in conjunction with a plane stop or bench dogs, you can secure virtually any size or shape workpiece anywhere on your bench. You can clamp parts equally well on the top or edge of a workbench. The low-profile design sits just 11/16 of an inch above the surface of your bench, so will not interfere when planning or sanding 3/4 inch thick material. The post is 2-5/8” long overall and can extend a full 2 inch into a benchtop. The moveable head travels on smooth-turning 10 TPI carbon steel threads that produce high horizontal clamping pressure with the least effort. The clamp head has a full 5” of travel. The handle is a low-profile sliding pin bar design. Threads and handle are zinc plated for smooth motion and corrosion resistance. The 2 inch long x 5/8 inch square moveable head has a V-groove in the center and pivots 2 degrees to accommodate irregular shaped parts. The head has 2 mounting holes to allow attachment of custom clamp pads. Both the head and post are black coated to resist corrosion. Will clamp parts up to 4 inches thick
