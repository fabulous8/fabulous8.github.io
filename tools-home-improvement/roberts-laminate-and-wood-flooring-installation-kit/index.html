ROBERTS Laminate and Floating Wood Flooring Installation Kit is designed for the do-it-yourself installer. It comes complete with a tapping block, pull bar and 50 wedge spacers. The wedge spacers can be stacked to provide the correct expansion gap at the perimeter of the room, while the tapping block and pull bar help make the installation a tight fit. This installation kit conveniently includes instructions on how to install laminate and floating wood flooring. For larger installations, multiple rooms or for the professional installer, you may want to upgrade to the Professional Pull Bar (model 10-18-8) and Professional Tapping Block (model 10-17-8), also available at Amazon.
