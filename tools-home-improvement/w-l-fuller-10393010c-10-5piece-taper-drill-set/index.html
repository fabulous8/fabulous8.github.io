From the Manufacturer W. L Fuller, Inc. has been manufacturing, recommending, supplying, and distributing tools to cut woods, plastics and metals for over 60 years. They produce the best quality tools made in the USA to predrill, countersink, counterbore and plug for screws and bolts of all different sizes and shapes.
