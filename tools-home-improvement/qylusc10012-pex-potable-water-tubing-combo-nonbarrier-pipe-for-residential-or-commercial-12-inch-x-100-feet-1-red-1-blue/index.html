Pexflow Potable Water Tubing (Non-Barrier PEX Pipe) is used for residential and commercial hot and cold water plumbing applications. Made of “Silane” or “Moisture Cure” method of cross-linking where links between the molecules of the HDPE polymer are formed after the extrusion process. The color of red tubing simply helps identify the pipe as hot water line in PEX plumbing distribution systems that connects to hot water heater. The color of blue tubing helps identify the pipe as cold water line in PEX plumbing systems. PEX tubing is resistance to temperature fluctuation all year around and provides peace of mind ensuring maintenance free for exceptional performance. For optimal leak free water flow connections, the tubing system works with cinch clamps seal accessories ensuring continuous performance with superior temperature and pressure rating. Ideal for indoor or outdoor plumbing that includes washing machine and garden applications. It is economical, safe and easily installed in any type of environment. This type of PEX is most common in the industry of water plumbing. Tubing is marked every 5 Feet and NSF certified for potable water use, lead-free and meets or exceeds the ASTM F2023 standard.
