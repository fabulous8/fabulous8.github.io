Hubbell Kellems support grips are used to hold the weight of electrical cable as it hangs in a vertical, sloping or horizontal position. Electrical cable must be supported, or its dead weight can cause excessive strain or pullout at the connections resulting in power failure. Support grips also absorb additional strain from flexure, vibration, expansion and contraction. Tin-coated bronze wire is corrosion resistant for normal outside areas. Solid eye assemblies provide eye reinforcement at support hardware. Single eye for single hook attachment of permanent indoor/outdoor cable. The strand equalizer positions wires for equal loading throughout the entire grip length. The endless weave provides easy installation onto cable and can be easily repositioned. CSA certified.
