D-C-Fix transparent self-adhesive window films are a collection of affordable window covering solutions which add design and interest to windows and glass surfaces while providing privacy and UV protection. D-C-Fix’s window films adhere to glass using a solvent free adhesive and are easily removable. Express your individual design ideas and improve privacy quickly and easily by applying the film directly to almost any glass surface for instant, beautiful and long lasting results. Application is easy: 1) Cut the film to the required size. 2) Spray the glass surface with plenty of water. 3) Gently remove the backing paper. 4) Apply to the glass surface using a D-C-Fix smoother or squeegee to remove excess water and air bubbles. D-C-Fix window films come in a wide assortment of patterns and designs to add style to your home while filtering up to 95% of harmful UV rays and reducing glare. They are ideal for patio doors and exterior windows as well as interior glass surfaces, mirrors and shower glass.
