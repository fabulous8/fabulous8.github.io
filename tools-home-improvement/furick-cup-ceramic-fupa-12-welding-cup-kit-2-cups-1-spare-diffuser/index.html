The FUPA Ceramic Cup comes with two cups and a spare diffuser. The Furick FUPA #12 covered glass covered welding cup uses anywhere from 25-30+ CFH of argon flow depending on the application. It is recommended with all the BIG Diffuser Cups that you start off on low amp settings to stabilize the arc, making sure you will not arc off the diffuser.
