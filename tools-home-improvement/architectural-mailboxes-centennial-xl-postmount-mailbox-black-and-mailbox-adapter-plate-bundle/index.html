Named “Centennial”, it represents the next generation of mailbox size, since the last traditional size was introduced over 100 years ago in 1915. Meeting the needs of today’s consumer, its ample mailbox compartment is designed to receive not only mail and magazines but multiple parcels. This means no more missed deliveries or parcels left out on the porch exposed to weather and thieves. Made with quality construction and heavy gauge materials complete with a die cast aluminum handle and rigid, powder coated steel flag.
