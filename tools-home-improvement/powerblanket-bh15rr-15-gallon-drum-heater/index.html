BH15RR: The 15 gallon barrel and drum heater from Powerblanket offers a state-of-the-art heating system that is far superior to band heaters, which localize the heat to only one spot. The Powerblanket drum heater encases the entire drum in a heating blanket, insulating the entire drum and distributing the heat across the entire surface. The BH15RR drum heater runs at 320 watts so that things warm up quickly, then it automatically cuts back to only 160 watts to maintain the temperature. It is water resistant, and can be used on poly and steel drums. Another great feature is the preset thermostat which limits the maximum temperature of the contents to approximately 100 ºF. The BH15RR drum heater is very easy to install, and is competitively priced. It is also ETL certified to UL & CSA safety standards.
