Crown Tools 375 / Big Horn 20182 2-1/2 Inch x 5 Inch flat cabinet scraper cuts faster than sandpaper but leaves a fine surface on hardwood. It is great for final smoothing and removing milling marks. These are commonly used by cabinetmakers and woodworkers to remove very small amounts of wood in order to create a shiny, level surface. Cabinet scrapers can also be used to remove paint, glue or stain from wood surfaces. When using a cabinet scraper, it is important to scrape along the grain of the wood in order to prevent the smooth surface from being damaged.
