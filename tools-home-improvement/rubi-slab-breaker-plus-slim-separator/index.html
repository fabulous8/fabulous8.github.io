The SLIM separator is the perfect complement for the cutting of ceramic sheets with SLIM CUTTER when the sheet thicknesses are equal to or greater than 8 mm. In addition to its main use (ceramic sheet cutting), the SLIM separator can also be used as an auxiliary separator for large format tiles. The SLIM separator is equipped with an ergonomic handle, SOFT-GRIP, to minimize user effort during use. Taking into account that, when cutting large-format pieces, it is very common and necessary to obtain narrow pieces or strips, in RUBI we have equipped the SLIM breaker with a system for adjusting the width of the piece, which allows us to obtain properly distributed, uniform, equitable and symmetrical braking power. Manufactured in high-performance steel sheets, the SLIM separator has a net weight of approximately 1.5 kg, which together with its small dimensions (23 cm x 18 cm) make it the perfect cutting complement for all professionals.
