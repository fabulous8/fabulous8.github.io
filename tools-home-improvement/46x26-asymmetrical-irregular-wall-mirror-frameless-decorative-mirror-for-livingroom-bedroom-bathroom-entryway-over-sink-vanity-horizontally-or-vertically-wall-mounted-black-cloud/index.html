The installation of an wall mirror typically involves the following steps: 1. Measuring and marking: Measure the area where the mirror will be installed and mark the location on the wall with a pencil. This will help you ensure that the mirror is level and centered. 2. Preparing the wall: Clean the area where the mirror will be installed to ensure a secure and stable installation. If the wall is drywall, you may want to add a wooden backing to the area to provide extra support. 3. Hanging the mirror: Hold the mirror in place and use a level to ensure it is straight. Then use a pencil to mark the location of the hanging hardware on the back of the mirror. Use a drill to attach the hanging hardware to the wall according to the manufacturer’s instructions. 4. Securing the mirror: Carefully lift the mirror and hang it on the wall. Check to make sure it is level and centered. If you have a heavy mirror, use a helper or a pulley system to help you hang it safely.
