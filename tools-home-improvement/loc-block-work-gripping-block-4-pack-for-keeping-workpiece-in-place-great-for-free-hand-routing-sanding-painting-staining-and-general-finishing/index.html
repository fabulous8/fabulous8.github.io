When performing simple tasks like rounding over the edge of a wood panel with your router or sanding a piece of wood with a belt or hand sander we need to keep the workpiece stationery. Our one of a kind Loc-Blocks make these jobs easy by gripping both your work surface as well as your project without the use of any clamps. This makes them perfect for sanding, drilling, routing, finishing and a whole lot more! Not only do our Loc-Blocks hold your workpiece still, they also function as a project support by elevating your work, which provides easy access to every edge of your workpiece. This is great for finishing your projects with stain, paint, varnish and more. What makes our Loc-Blocks work? The soft, receptive, anti-vibration, non-slip rubber pads on both the top and bottom of the blocks. These pads not only grip your work surface and workpiece, the pads also protect and will not mark or mar your wood.
