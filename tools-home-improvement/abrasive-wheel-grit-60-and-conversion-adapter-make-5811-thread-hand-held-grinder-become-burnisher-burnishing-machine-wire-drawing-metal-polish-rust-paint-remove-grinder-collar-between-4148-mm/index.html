This burnishing wheel is standard sized wheel (they fit standard burnisher). Please 100% make sure your grinder’s or polisher’s spindle is 5/8"-11 Thread. Please kindly refer to the 6 photo attached. Please 100% make sure your grinder’s or polisher’s head collar diameter is between 41 and 48 mm. if less than 41 MM or more than 48 MM, the burnisher wheel protection cover does not fit. (c) Description: This adapter attachment allows you to easily turn the angle grinder or wet polishing tool that you already own into a burnisher.
