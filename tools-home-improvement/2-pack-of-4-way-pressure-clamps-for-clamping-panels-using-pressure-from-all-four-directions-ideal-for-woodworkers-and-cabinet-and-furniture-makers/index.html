Clamping panels doesn’t have to be difficult. This clamp system is designed for the sole purpose of clamping panels using pressure from the top, bottom and sides all at the same time! Clamping Panels Can Certainly Be A Challenge. Especially When We Try To Use Everyday Pipe Clamps Or Bar Clamps. While These Types Of Clamps Work Fairly Well On Certain Glue Up Projects, The One Thing They Don’T Do At All Is Apply Pressure From The Top And Bottom Of The Panels During Glue Up. In Fact, With All The Pressure Being Applied On Both Sides, Bar And Pipe Clamps Will Almost Certainly Bow, Buckle Or Rack Your Panel Glue Up Project Let’S Make Racked Panel Glue Ups A Thing Of The Past. With A Simple Turn Of The Rotating Clamp Handle, All Four Sides Of The Clamp Will Draw Inward At The Same Time Build Any Length Clamp With The Durable Plastic Bars With 4 Notches For Maximum Adjustability To Handle A Variety Of Stock Widths.
