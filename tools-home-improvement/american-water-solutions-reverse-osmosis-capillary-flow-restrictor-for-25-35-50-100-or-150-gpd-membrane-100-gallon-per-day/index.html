This listing is for one (1) Reverse Osmosis Capillary Flow Restrictor. The Capillary Flow Restrictor is to be insterted into a 1/4 inch tubing and is inserted into the tubing that goes to the drain for your waste water. Each Flow Restrictor is sized to match the specific gallon per day of your Reverse Osmosis Membrane. Be certain to check the Gallon per day “GPD” of your membrane to ensure correct Capillary Flow Restrictor as you can prematurely foul the membrane is your flow restrictor is too small.
