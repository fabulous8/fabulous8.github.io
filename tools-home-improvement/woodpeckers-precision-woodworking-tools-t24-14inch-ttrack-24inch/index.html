Compatible with standard 1/4-inch hardware Unique outside profile allows for better fit in undersized grooves Track is punched on 4-inch centers and is available in 24-inch, 36-inch and 48-inch lengths Made in USA Over 30 years in the tool making experience

Woodpecker’s T-Track has a number of nice features including a unique outside profile, a recessed channel for pan-head screws, and compatibility with standard 1/4-inch hardware. Its unique outside shape allows for a better fit in undersized grooves. The profile is slightly tapered being wider at the top then the bottom thus ensuring easy installation in saw cut dados which may not have crisp, square inside corners. Inside profile is compatible with standard 1/4-20 nuts and bolts as well as other accessories like a stop block, edge-clip and rectangular nuts. Woodpeckers 1/4-inch T-Block, (#T14TSTOP) fits flush or slightly below the top of this track. This track is punched on 4 inch centers and includes #6 x 1/2-inch pan head screws, however longer screws or construction adhesive can also be used.
