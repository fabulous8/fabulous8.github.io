The Advanced European Training System is an expert tool designed to teach horses to engage and move forward, enhancing their carriage, collection, and headset. By setting more rigid boundaries, it encourages horses to move away from pressure. This system also aids in strengthening and increasing flexibility in key areas like the shoulders, neck, back, and hind quarters. Made with soft rope, pulleys, and fleece-covered elastic cord breeching, it is best used with light pressure until the horse gains confidence in this style of rigging. Note: This is not for beginners - the horse should be experienced with ground driving and the handler should understand its principles. Surcingle, lunging caveson, and bridle are sold separately.
