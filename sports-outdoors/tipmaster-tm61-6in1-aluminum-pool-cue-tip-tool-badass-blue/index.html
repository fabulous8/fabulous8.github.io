Get everything you need to play your best game of pool with the TipMaster TM61 6-in-1 aluminum tip tool. Replacement abrasive pads are also available. TipMaster 6-in-1 Tip Tool Features Tip Tapper - Creates pockets and scuffs on your pool cue tip surface for increased chalk absorption. This tool can also be used to scuff a badly glazed tip. Dime Radius Shaper - Shapes and scuffs your pool cue tip to a perfect dime (9.1mm) radius. Nickel Radius Shaper - Shapes and scuffs your pool cue tip to a perfect nickel (10.5mm) radius. Tip Burnisher - Hardens and polishes the sides of your pool cue tip flush with the ferrule to reduce mushrooming.
