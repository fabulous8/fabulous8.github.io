1050 ballistic fabric provides weatherproof protection for your SUP paddle Low-density foam cushions your paddle Tension lock straps adjust for any paddle length External mesh zipper pocket provides extra storage for your gear

The centerpiece of this innovative bag is its multi-component system. A 1050 ballistic-laminated, foam-lined tube houses the handle of the paddle, while a foam-lined, ballistic-laminated pouch protects the blade. The two pieces are locked together with an adjustable-length tension lock strapping system that easily accommodates paddles of any length. One strap can be cinched down, serving to hold the components together, while the other can be loosened for use as a shoulder strap. The exterior of the blade pouch features a generously sized mesh zipper pocket, and we take it one step further with bungee lattice on the exterior of the pocket for even more storage. All the materials used in this SUP paddle bag are industrial strength and weatherproof, meaning you’ll be able to tote your paddle to and from the beach for years to come.
