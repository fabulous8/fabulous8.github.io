The Waldner 900 is equipped with real competition wood, which gives maximum fun and max. performance qualities. Also adopted from the competition technology is the “ERGO handle” which means the transition from the blade to the handle is fluent and there are no edges that will cause pressure points on the index finger. The Bat sits perfectly in the hand and the switch from forehand to backhand (or vice versa) can be achieved as quickly as possible, a clear advantage in such a fast-paced sport as table tennis.
