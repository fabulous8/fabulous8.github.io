Softcover set of 6 preschool learning aids: Going On Eagerly, Hearing and Helping, Inside and Outside, Just Thinking and Choosing, Keep Doing it Carefully, and Learning More and More Each book aims to teach children to be careful and neat and to take care of books properly Designed to help your child practice shapes, word meanings, and basic numbers Coloring pages help children learn to color within the lines Excellent preschool aids for children three years old and up

“Going on Eagerly,” with a polar theme, gives a basic introduction to the series. Exercises include using eight colors, working with four basic shapes, writing and counting with numbers 0 through 10, identifying sequence, drawing missing parts and simple pictures. Hearing and Helping, with a rain forest theme, includes practice in hearing initial consonant sounds and rhyming words; understanding words in sentence context, as well as following instructions. Exercises also include examples of families and children helping others. Inside and Outside has a woodlands theme and aims to teach neatness and carefulness in use and care of books, outdoor weather as sunny, rainy, windy, cloudy, and snowy, to introduce the thermometer used to measure temperatures, and more. “Just Thinking and Choosing” has a mountain theme and seeks to help children identify opposites such as in and out, on and off, large and small, practice helpfulness and tidiness in daily jobs, health, and safety, recognize sequence of repeated shapes and pictures, and more. “Keep Doing it Carefully” has a deserts and grasslands theme and aims to teach children to count and identify numbers 0 to 20, write numbers after, between, and before 1 through 10, write capital and small alphabet letters correctly and some words, and more. “Learning More and More” has an oceans theme and will assist children in reviewing and using eleven colors: eight basic colors, white, pink, and gray, mixing the three primary colors to make other colors, learning about birds and animals that live in or near the oceans, and more.
