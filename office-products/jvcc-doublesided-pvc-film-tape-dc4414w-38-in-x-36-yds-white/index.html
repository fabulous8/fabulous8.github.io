JVCC DC-4414W Double-Sided PVC Film Tape is a white PVC film coated on both sides (AKA double-coated) with a solvent-based acrylic adhesive. Extremely aggressive adhesive will bond to nearly all materials (has just one minute dwell time). It has excellent temperature, UV, aging, and water vapor resistance. The tape comes on a gold siliconized paper release liner. JVCC DC-4414W double-sided tape is used for secure attachment of extruded plastic profiles in furniture and general industries and to mount signs and large format graphics. Also recommended for bonding trims in automotive and other industries. Carrier/Backing: white polyvinyl chloride (PVC) film. Adhesive: solvent-based acrylic. Release Liner: gold siliconized paper.
