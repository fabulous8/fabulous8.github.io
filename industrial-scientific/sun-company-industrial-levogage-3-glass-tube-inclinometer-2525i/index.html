Inclinometers go by many names: Tilt gauge, angle indicator, tilt indicator, level, level indicator, level gauge, angle gauge, tilt meter, gradiometer, grade indicator, percent slope meter, ball bank indicator, or clinometer and heel gauge (two common terms in the Marine or sailing industry).
