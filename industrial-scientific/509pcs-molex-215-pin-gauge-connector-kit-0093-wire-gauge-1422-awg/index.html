Overview Standard .093" pin and socket power connectors from Molex provide a flexible design choice for medium-power applications. Molex’s Standard .093" pin and socket power connectors are an industry standard connector system based on an .093" pin diameter. Molex’s 2.36mm diameter terminals are used in the 5.03mm pitch Standard .093" and 6.71mm pitch 3191 products. The terminals can be used in either crimp housings or crimp receptacles and are capable of carrying up to 14.0A (9.0A with the largest circuit size housings; 17.0A with high-current/low-force terminals). The system is available in wire-to-wire and wire-to-board configurations, up to 15 circuits.
