Black Metal Gridwall Sign Holder, Card Grip for Gridwall & Slat Grid Panels - Single Unit
$7.95
Check on amazon

Place these metal sign holders onto your gridwall display for a simple & effective way to hold up signs on your grid panel or fixture. The sign holder is L shaped so you can rest it on the top part of the square of your standard 3" gridwall panel. Simply slide signs or cards into the round tension ring to display your signs. They feature a flexible metal tab on the bottom that can be bent over the bottom square of the grid for additional stability. Use more than one sign holder to display wider signs. Our sign holders are made from a heavy duty metal and are available in a black finish. The entire unit measures 5.5" H x 1" W. The metal tab measures 1" H and the ring diameter is .8". 1 sign holder is included in this order. Works great for signs at least .035 inches thick.
