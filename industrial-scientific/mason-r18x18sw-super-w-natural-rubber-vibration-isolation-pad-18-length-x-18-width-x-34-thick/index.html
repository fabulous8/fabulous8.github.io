This Mason Industries R18X18SW sheet consists of 81 2x2" Super W vibration isolation pads that reduce noise, high frequency vibration, and impact from machines. These vibration isolation pads are made of natural rubber for durability and maximum vibration isolation. The waffle pattern forms a self-sealing perimeter to keep oil and fluids from contaminating the pad, and the material contains anti-oxidants and anti-ozonants for corrosion resistance. A thin web separates the pads to provide evenly dimensioned sections that can be placed side-by-side to accommodate a machine’s load or stacked with 16 gauge shims between layers for increased deflection. The matching pattern on both sides of the pad improves its structural strength for increased load capacity. These Mason Super W pads are suitable for use with compressors, HVAC units, pumps, generators, transformers, and presses.
