Blind rivet has 2 parts: the rivet and the mandrel. The rivet fastens to the materials, and the mandrel is used to set the rivet and then is discarded. Blind rivets differ from solid rivets in their ability to be fully installed in a joint on just one side of a structure, where it is “blind” to the opposing side.
