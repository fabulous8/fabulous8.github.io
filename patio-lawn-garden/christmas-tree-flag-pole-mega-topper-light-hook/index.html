Standard Flagpole Installation:

  1. Clamping Adapter Not Needed
  • Skip using the included adapter; it’s not required for Standard Pole setup.
  1. Secure Christmas Tree Topper:
  • Locate the 2 top eyelets on the Standard Pole.
  • Tie a sturdy rope between these eyelets.
  1. Attach the Christmas Tree Topper:
  • Clip the carabiner to the middle of the rope.
  • Secure the Christmas Tree Topper to the carabiner.
  1. Illuminate with Lights:
  • Place Christmas Lights between the teeth of the Christmas Tree Mega Topper and the Hoist.
  • Ensure the lights are evenly distributed.
  1. Adjust Height:
  • Lift or lower the ring until the Christmas Tree Topper is at your desired height.
  1. Secure Lights in Place:
  • Stretch out each light strand and use stakes to anchor them, keeping the lights in place.

Telescoping Pole Installation:

  1. Use the Clamping Adapter:
  • Utilize the Clamping Adapter included in the package for the Telescoping Pole.
  1. Adjust the Pole Height:
  • Collapse the Telescoping Pole.
  • Clamp the Christmas Tree Mega Topper at the topmost section or at your preferred height using the Clamping Adapter.
  1. Install Christmas Lights:
  • Position the Christmas Lights between the teeth of the Christmas Tree Mega Topper and the pole.
  • Ensure the lights are evenly spread.
  1. Raise the Christmas Tree Topper:
  • Lift the Telescoping Pole, raising the Christmas Tree Mega Topper to the desired height.
  1. Secure Lights in Place:
  • Stretch out each light strand and use stakes to anchor them, keeping the lights securely in place.
