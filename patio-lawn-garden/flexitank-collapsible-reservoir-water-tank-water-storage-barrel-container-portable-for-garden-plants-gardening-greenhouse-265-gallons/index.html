Essential Tips for FlexiTanks - 1: With any soft-bodied reservoir, placement is critical, so ensure you set up the FlexiTank on a flat, level surface. Uneven surfaces can cause punctures on the bottom of the tank. 2: NEVER move the FlexiTank with any amount of water in it. Like any collapsible reservoir, it is a seamed tank, so pulling at the sides/dragging the tank with water weight in it will separate the seams and may create a leak overtime. 3: Place the support band one inch above the FlexiTank Tap. This ensures the seams don’t bulge and open from the volume of water in the tank. Place support band on FlexiTank before you fill it up and adjust to the correct position as the tank is filling. Improper seam placement may create leaks at the bottom seam!
