There are several ways to attract owls to your yard or property: Provide a suitable habitat: Owls need a safe and comfortable place to live and hunt. Plant native trees and shrubs that provide cover, nesting sites, and food for owls. Offer a reliable food source: Owls rely on small rodents as their primary food source. If you have a rodent problem on your property, owls may be attracted to hunt them. Use a bird feeder: Supplement the natural food sources in your yard by placing a bird feeder filled with owl-friendly treats such as suet, dried mealworms, and seeds. Make your yard quiet: Owls rely on their keen hearing to locate prey, so a quiet yard is more attractive to them. Avoid using loud machinery or music that might disrupt their hunting. Install a nest box: If you have a suitable habitat and food sources, you can attract owls to your yard by installing a nest box specifically designed for owls. These boxes should be placed in a location with good visibility, and away from human activity.
