The traditional elegance of the CUBICO Cottage offers a classic cubic shape that can make a bold statement or fit seamlessly into any decor. Available as 22 or 30 inches tall, in 4 colors, with a beautiful wicker finish, this planter accents your home’s indoor and outdoor furnishings.
