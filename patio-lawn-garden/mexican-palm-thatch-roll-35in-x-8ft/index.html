Tropical decorative roofing treatment

Mexican Palm Thatch Roll is a natural product, hand woven from palm leaf strips. Replenish able, renewable, Eco-friendly resource A simple approach to create an authentic palm thatch look. Used as a covering for Tiki huts, pergolas and gazebos or hang as an accent. Mexican rain cape is not waterproof, it is purely for shade covering or decorative purposes. Lasts outdoors, approx. 1-2 years in wet climates, double in dry climates. Will last indefinitely undercover or indoors. Each piece covers 20 sq ft. The length of the thatch is 30” of the woven area and fringe is approx. 5”. Mexican rain cape installation is very simple and doesn’t require any special skills. Heavy duty staples or zip ties work the best when attaching the thatch to the tiki roof. The thatch can also by installed by stapling to battens or a plywood subroof. Minimum suggested pitch is 30 degrees, steeper pitch increases longevity. For subsequent courses, allow the fringe of the next panels to overhang the panel in the preceding course. Panels are butted side to side. This is a very versatile thatch and can be cut into any shape by simply cutting between the knots without unraveling. Fire Retardant is available for this product.
