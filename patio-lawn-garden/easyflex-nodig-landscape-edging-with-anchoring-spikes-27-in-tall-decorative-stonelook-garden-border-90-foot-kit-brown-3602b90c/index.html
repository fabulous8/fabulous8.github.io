The EasyFlex Decorative Faux Stone No-Dig Landscape Edging Kit adds an rustic finish to any gardening or landscaping project. The brown ornamental edging is flexible enough to use as a tree ring and the patented No-Dig L-shaped design eliminates damage from digging into tree and plant roots. An overlapping post system creates seamless borders, eliminating the need for connector pieces. Unlike other decorative fence-style edging products, the 2.7 inch high edging has a built-in retaining section designed to hold back bedding materials and can be installed on top of landscape fabric to prevent weed growth.
