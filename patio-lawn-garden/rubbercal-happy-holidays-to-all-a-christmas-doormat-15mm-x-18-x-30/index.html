A charming Christmas doormat is just the thing to complete your home’s holiday aesthetic this year. This holiday doormat features natural coir fibers for durability and simple script that reads “Happy Holidays.” The simplicity in design is perfect to complement any holiday décor, from elaborate Christmas lights to a classic wreath on the front door. The greeting on this festive coir doormat is painted with waterproof emulsion paint, similar to the type that is used on walls, to ensure the design does not bleed or rub off. You will be able to wipe your feet on this coir mat in confidence, knowing the design can withstand the physical abuse. Combined with the extreme durability of coir fiber, this welcome doormat will last you all season long! Coir is a natural material that is harvested from the husks of coconuts, and as a result coir fiber mats can withstand temperature, dampness, and year-round weather conditions. Heavy rain and snowfall are no match for this holiday mat, which is backed with watertight PVC to contain excess dampness until it dries. Coir doormats can even withstand ozone and UV rays without discoloring or taking damage, making them perfect for a home in any region. Coir bristles are also very rigid and function well as a boot and shoe scraper, trapping excess dirt on the mat until it can be properly cleaned. Cleaning this Christmas welcome mat is also extremely easy – simply give the mat a good shake outdoors, or use a vacuum for an even deeper cleaning.
