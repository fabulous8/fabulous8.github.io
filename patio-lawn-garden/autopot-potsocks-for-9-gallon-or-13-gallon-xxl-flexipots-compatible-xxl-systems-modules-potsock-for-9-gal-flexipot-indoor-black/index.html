The PotSock for 9 gal or 13 gal FlexiPot fits the fabric pots used in XXL and Auto5 modules and systems. Alternatively they can be used with 9 gal or 13 gal FlexiPots in open garden trays. These securely stitched root control covers have been designed to keep even the most agile root system where it belongs…contained in the pot! PotSocks are great for growers cultivating long term crops or simply plants with a very aggressive root system. By containing the roots PotSocks ensure that the AQUAvalve in your module tray operates without interference, giving your plants exactly the levels of water and nutrient solution they require. PotSocks will help prevent roots from growing into the tray and affecting the AQUAvalve but it is important to always check your AQUAvalves on a regular basis as aggressively rooting plants can still grow through on rare occasions. If you have an aggressively rooting plant, consider doubling up on PotSocks to add an extra layer of protection.
