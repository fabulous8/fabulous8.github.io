Laser Engraved Made from 100% coir (coconut fiber) Non-Slip rubber backing Measures 30” x 18” x 5/8” thickness, not a tripping hazard.

Laser engraved mats have the design burned into the coconut fibers with a laser engraving machine (no paints are used), producing a dark brown tonal color.
