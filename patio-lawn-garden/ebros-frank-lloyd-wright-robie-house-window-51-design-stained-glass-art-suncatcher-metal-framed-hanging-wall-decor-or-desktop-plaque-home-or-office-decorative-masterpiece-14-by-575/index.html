The Frank Lloyd Wright Robie Window Stained Glass is inspired by a garage window design in the Frederic Robie House in Chicago, Illinois. The geometric lines of this Frank Lloyd Wright window stained glass reflect other accents Wright designed throughout the house. Their color and delicate geometric diamond and chevron shapes are abstractions of the surrounding foliage. On this glass panel, enamel colors are individually applied to a single sheet of glass which is then kiln fired to permanently fuse the enamels to the glass. The glass is then metal framed and includes both a hanging chain and a wooden plaque base. The measurements of this beautiful Frank Lloyd Wright Glass art are: GLASS: 14" by 5.75"" Wide x 0.25" Thick.
