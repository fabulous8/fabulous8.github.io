DIG’s drip irrigation hole punches are designed to easily punch a clean hole in any size of drip tubing for the insertion of drip emitters, micro sprinklers, and 1/4 in. barbed fittings. DIG’s punches are designed to remove the residual debris formed as the hole is created in the drip tubing, protecting the drip emitters and micro sprinklers from plastic debris that over time can plug the system. The small punch is inexpensive and the deluxe gun punch is heavy duty with optional replacement of the stainless steel pin.
