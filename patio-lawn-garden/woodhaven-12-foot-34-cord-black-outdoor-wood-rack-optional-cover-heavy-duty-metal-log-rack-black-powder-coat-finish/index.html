The Woodhaven 12 foot black 3/4 cord outdoor firewood racks are a great addition to anyone who enjoys burning a large amount of firewood year round. Its heavy-duty construction features hand arc welded end sections and laser precise drilled holes. This outdoor log holder is great for storing the largest wood piles outside in a clean and professional manner. The Woodhaven black 12 foot firewood rack comes with: 2 welded uprights, 6 four foot drilled bottom tubes and 6 connecting brackets. Nuts and bolts are also included in each package. The rack comes with a 7/16 inch wrench. For assembly a rubber mallet is recommended if bolts are a little hard to insert. This 12 foot black firewood rack does feature a seasoning cover. Making it perfect for those looking to place this log rack in an area that is exposed to the elements. The seasoning cover allows the top level of wood dry while exposing the majority of wood to the sun and wind. This promotes curing and better burning firewood. The volume of this firewood rack is figured based on 24 inch long log splits. Cover Imported.
