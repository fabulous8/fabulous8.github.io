Our Nesting Perch Birdhouse is popular with species of birds that prefer an open-front nesting box. Song sparrows, catbirds, thrashers, phoebes, barn swallows and robins like to raise their families in a platform nest. This easy-to-install perch may be located under eaves, inside a garage or barn or on the side of a building. It offers protection from the elements and requires no maintenance. Mounts by putting two screws through the pre-drilled holes in the back of the Nesting Perch. Hand crafted in Maine by Coveside, of sustainably grown Eastern White Pine.
