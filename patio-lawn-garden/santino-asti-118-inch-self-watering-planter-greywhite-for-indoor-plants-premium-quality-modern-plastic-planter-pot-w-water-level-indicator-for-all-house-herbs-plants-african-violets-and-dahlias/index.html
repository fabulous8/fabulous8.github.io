Santino pots are great for an outdoor garden, balcony, indoor decoration, greening projects etc. HOW TO USE: 1. Before planting the plant, it is recommended to pour a layer of gravel (2-3cm/1inch) for ideal moisture balance. You can also use perlite or specific mixtures of these materials. 2.
