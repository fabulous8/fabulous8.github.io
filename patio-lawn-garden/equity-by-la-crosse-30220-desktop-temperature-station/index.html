Equity by La Crosse 30220 Desktop Temperature Station

Indoor Temperature (°F/°C) & Indoor Humidity Weather Forecast Records Min/Max Records Alarm With Snooze Bright Back Light

Monitor a room’s climate while keeping an eye on the time with this digital desktop station from Equity by La Crosse. To inform the user of indoor climate conditions, the desktop station assesses indoor temperature and humidity, records minimum/maximum readings, follows temperature trends, and anticipates forecast and comfort level with graphic indicators. Tracking these conditions allows users to make environmental adjustments, such as employing a dehumidifier or turning up the thermostat, to achieve the most comfortable and healthy indoor atmosphere possible. Sleek in black plastic casing, the desktop station’s LCD screen relays the time in large black digits. Pressing the light button activates the blue backlight for on-demand screen illumination. Additionally, the desktop station keeps track of the date and the day of the week. For time alerts, the desktop station offers an alarm with a push-button eight-minute snooze function. The desktop station has a built-in stand for tabletop display, but also is equipped for wall mounting. Battery-operated, the separate purchase of two AAA batteries is required. A limited one-year warranty covers this item. The desktop station measures 3 inches wide by 1 inch deep by 5 inches high.
