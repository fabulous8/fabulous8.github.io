The Waterco Baker Hydro Skimmer One-Piece Deluxe with Control Plate is designed to provide the most efficient skimming action compared to conventional skimmers where water from the main drain does not pass through the skimmer basket, but the Waterco Baker Hydro Skimmer is designed to pass all water through the basket, making it easy to remove leaves and debris before going to the pump. This deluxe version includes a patented Hydro-Control plate to easily regulate the flow ratio between the main drain and surface scrape to provide a convenient connection point for a vacuum hose. It also has plumbing connections that can accept 1.5-inch and 2-inch pipes. The inlet fitting provides an easy-access connection for a vacuum cleaner. All connections are constructed of ABS plastic material and designed to allow bottom or side connection of the suction line for pumping. All ports internally threaded for easy pressure testing. The unique patented hydrodynamic weir in the skimmer creates a lifting action on the skimmer plate similar to an airplane wing. This uplift accelerates surface flow and eliminates ineffective deep currents. The Hydro-Skimmer skims a thinner layer of surface water at a higher rate, resulting in 300% to 500% more surface scraping, and does so at flow rates as low as 10 gallons per minute.
