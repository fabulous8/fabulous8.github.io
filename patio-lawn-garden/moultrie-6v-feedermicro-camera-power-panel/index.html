The number of days in which the field life of your game camera or feeder is extended will be dependent on model and the installation location of the Power Panel. Alkaline or lithium batteries should be installed in your Micro Game Camera when using the Power Panel. Rechargeable batteries should NOT be used in the Micro Game Cameras with this Power Panel. Moultrie Feeder kits require installation of a 6V 5 amp rechargeable battery in conjunction with the Power Panel. The power panel helps keep the feeder kit battery charged.
