Conventional gardening wastes both water resources and fertilizer, with runoff water washing away the nutrients from the potting soil, preventing the maximum absorption and utilization by the roots. This is why we created the EarthBag, the reservoir base that collects the nutrient rich runoff water, which is then recirculated back into the growing medium. The Earthbag system offers the most economical and efficient growing system to the gardener; while also being the first to incorporate a self-watering feature with a growbag. The permeable grow bag ensures oxygenation of the roots and fosters the healthy growth of the plant. The vents in the reservoir base as well as the breathable side walls enable air-pruning of the roots, which stimulates the constant growth of new and healthy branching roots, preventing root matting and clumping. The air-pruning of the roots stimulates new root tip formation due to increased oxygenation. The new root tips improve both water and nutrient absorption, thus resulting in healthy plant growth and improving harvest.
