Fiskars Lia Griffith Chevron Double Tag Maker with Built-in Eyelet Setter lets you quickly create error-free, chevron tags for gifts and more Signature Lia Griffith color and design is a stylish addition to your craft tool collection Includes 20 silver 3/16 in. eyelets (also accommodates 5/32 in. eyelets) Tag size: 1.3 in. x 1.8 in.; punches up to 80-lb. cardstock

Create large, chevron tags for embellishments, cards, home décor and more with the easy-to-use Fiskars Lia Griffith Chevron Double Tag Maker with Built-in Eyelet Setter. Featuring stylish design accents by internationally renowned DIY designer and maker, Lia Griffith, this tag maker makes it easy to create great-looking tags for gifts and other crafts. Simply insert your paper into the large slot and depress the lever to punch two tags at once. Next, insert the punched-out tag into the small slot and punch again for a perfectly centered hole. To complete your tag, drop the eyelet in the tag’s hole, slip the tag hole and eyelet over the peg on the back of the punch and depress the handle. The eyelet will be precisely set — no hammering required. It’s so easy, you’ll never have to waste craft materials or start over again because of misalignment or an offset eyelet.
