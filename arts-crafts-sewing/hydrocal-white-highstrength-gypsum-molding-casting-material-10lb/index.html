High-Strength Gypsum-Based casting material 6000 PSI strength when dry: resistant to breaking and chipping Very Fine-Grain: High-Detail Capture Possible Can be Carved or Added to After Drying Easy to Use: Mix By Weight, not Volume

Overview Teton Foundry Supply™ prepackaged Hydrocal®* White is a high-performance gypsum-based casting material that reaches an impressive 6000 psi strength when fully cured. Use it where fine-detail reproduction is desired along with chip-resistance. Supported Applications -Solid/Hollow casting of various decorative objects -Low-use foundry patterns and other similar tooling Uses -Modeling (scenery) and professional art applications -This product is NOT compatbile with molten metal (do not use as a mold for molten metal) -This product is NOT recommended for outdoor or wet area use. -DO NOT use this product to make castings of humans or live animals (severe burns possible). Directions -Mix 40 to 41 parts water per 100 parts Hydrocal (by weight) -Always add plaster to water, sift or break apart lumps in plaster before adding, and mix well. -Sets approximately 25-35 minutes after mixing -Clean tools prior to product setting or it may be hard to remove product from tools. Application Notes -Product may be carved and/or added to after curing (does not unduly dull tools) -Appropriate mold release should be used with all patterns to avoid sticking. -Generally, bubbles should be removed from the pattern surface to avoid visible defects in the casting (a damp paint brush under the surface of the wet plaster works for smaller items) -Voids from bubbles or other flaws may be patched with a small amount of new plaster as needed. Product Size/Volume/Weight Info -10lb of this product makes approximately 225 cubic inches of cast material (similar volume to a cube measuring 6"X6"X6") -Wet density is 110lb/cubic ft -Dry density is 90lb/cubic ft *=Hydrocal® is a registered trademark of USG corp.
