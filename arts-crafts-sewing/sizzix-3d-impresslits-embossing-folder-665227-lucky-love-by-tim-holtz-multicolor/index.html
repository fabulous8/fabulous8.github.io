All perfect die cutting and embossing machines for card making, so you won’t be short on projects you can utilise this embossing folder for! What is embossing and debossing? Embossing is the process of creating raised and textured designs in materials such as paper and cardstock. Debossing is the same idea, though instead of raising the material, it pushes the material downwards, to create a ‘sunken’ effect. Our 3-D Embossing Folders do both to create that 3-D effect! How do I use a 3-D Embossing Folder? It’s easy! Grab your Big Shot Machine and 1 Cutting Pad to get started. Firstly, for best results when using cardstock, spray fine water mist onto material before placing inside the folder, this will help to create an even deeper impression! Place the folder on top of your platform and complete the sandwich with your cutting pad placed on the top. With one roll through your die cutting machine, your 3-D design will be revealed!
