It’s never been easier and prettier to apply personalized messages to your cakes: With Sweet Stamp by AmyCakes you can easily emboss your message or name then paint — no more messing around with frustrating and time-consuming cutters or struggling to find modern and elegant lettering. Use with ganache, regular buttercream and fondant (sugarpaste). Each color-coded set comes in a handy storage tray (as seen in picture, with each letter, number or symbol fitting into its same-shape cavity) so you can keep track of your letters easily; using a blade or sticky tape, you can release the letters from the tray. If you need to use the same letter twice, simply measure the gap and apply it individually by hand afterward, or purchase an add-on pack of letters. Recommended for use are edible art paints or a mix of dust and dipping solution to paint. If you have the elegant or curly set, using a 00 brush is recommended. Take your time painting in your letters. It’s one of the more enjoyable tasks in cake decorating.
