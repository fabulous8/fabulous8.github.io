Seam binding that’s superior to bias tape 5/8" (16mm) Wide 20 meter roll (21.87 yards) Made in Italy Imported exclusively by Bias Bespoke Supply Co.

Juno seam binding tape is a true innovation. If you’ve ever used regular bias tape, you know it’s weaknesses: it changes width, getting narrower when applied to convex curves and wider on concave curves, and unless you make it yourself it never really looks luxe. Our Juno, however, is woven as a rigid fixed width tape, and becomes somewhat stretchy through a unique finishing process developed by the factory in Italy. It’s exactly stretchy enough to easily and beautifully mold itself to more extreme curves than regular bias tape allows without ‘breaking’ (creating dimples), and while still maintaining it’s width. To top it off, it looks lovely. One side of the tape has a nice matte finish and the other is shiny. Though the shine is present on all the colors, it’s definitely more noticeable on the the dark colors.
