All perfect die cutting and embossing machines for card making, so you won’t be short on projects you can utilise this embossing folder for! What is embossing and debossing? Embossing is the process of creating raised and textured designs in materials such as paper and cardstock. Debossing is the same idea, though instead of raising the material, it pushes the material downwards, to create a ‘sunken’ effect. Our 3D Embossing Folders do both to create that 3D effect! How do I use a 3D Embossing Folder? It’s easy! Grab your Big Shot Machine (660200) and Standard Cutting Pads (655093) to get started. Firstly, place your chosen paper or cardstock inside the folder, for best results when using cardstock, spray a little water on the material before placing inside the folder. This will help to create an even deeper impression! Place the folder on top of your first cutting pad and complete the sandwich with your second cutting pad placed on the top. With one roll through your die cutting machine, your 3D design will be revealed!
