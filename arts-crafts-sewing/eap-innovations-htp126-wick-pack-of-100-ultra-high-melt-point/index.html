Designed to provide benefits of a self-trimming wick posture with rigidity approaching those found in cored wicks. Well-suited for tough-to-burn formulations that contain many additives. Excellent for paraffin, vegetable and gel candle systems.

HTP Wicks- HTP Wicks are composed of high-performance fibers and other natural materials engineered in a specially constructed braid, which produces a more predictable wick posture. This composition provides the benefits of a self-trimming wick posture, but also features rigidity comparable to those found in cored wicks. While HTP wicks are well-suited for tough-to-burn- formulations in which may contain many additives, they also serve as an excellent choice of wick for paraffin, vegetable, and gel candle systems.
