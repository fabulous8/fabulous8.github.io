Millefiori Glass Beads - Millefiori is Italian for “one thousand flowers.” Millefiori glass beads are created using different colors of glass rods (canes) that are bundled together to form a mosaic design. The bundle is then fused together and slices are cut off and melted over a glass core, giving the bead a three-dimensional look. The charming and colorful flower designs within millefiori glass beads are sure to inspire and make them a popular choice for festive and whimsical jewelry.
