Eco-Flo All-In-One Stain & Finish is color and finish combined in one easy-to-use product. It is a water based, low V.O.C., stain for use on natural veg-tanned tooling leather. Perfect to use when speed is an issue or for beginners or kids groups. Directions for use: Shake well before use. Transfer a liberal amount of All-In-One to a dry piece of sheep wool or soft cloth. Apply to leather quickly, rubbing in a circular motion until color is even across the project and all cuts and impressions are full. Remove excess All-In-One immediately with a clean piece of sheep wool or soft cloth. Allow to dry completely, then buff to a mellow glow. Only one coat is necessary. A second coat usually won’t go on smoothly, so be sure the first coat is heavy, then wipe off only as much until the desired color is reached.
