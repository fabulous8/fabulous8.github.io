Casting molds are a simple way to make beautiful glass art. This mold allows you to create multiple pieces in just one firing. These pieces are beautiful enough to stand alone or to incorporate into larger projects. Molds can be filled with glass frit, powder, or sheet glass. Mold measures 6-1/2" x 8". Flower castings measure approximately 3" and 3-1/2" with a fill weight of 30 and 35 grams. Leaf castings measure approximately 2" with a fill weight of 15 grams. Images courtesy of Creative Paradise.
