The Ultimate Bench Anvil Combination Kit includes: 1 ring mandrel, 2 different style wooden bench pins 2 bracelet mandrels, a round and an oval, 1 bezel mandrel adapter 3 bezel mandrels,1 round,1 oval, and 1 square. 1 bench mounting system which has a flat anvil surface on top The anvil top offers approximately 4 1/2 x 2 3/4 inches of work space. The bench anvil kit can clamp to a table up to 2.25” (5.7 cm) thick. The ring mandrel is hardened steel, perfect for forming and hammering on, and has U.S. sizes 1-15 marked on it. It is 8.75” (22.2 cm) long plus the tang and is secured into the system with a thumb screw. The metal bezel mandrel adapter fits into the system to hold a bezel mandrel securely while you work. It has two different size holes for mandrel placement. One opening is approx. 12.39 MM, and fits our bezel mandrels. And the other is approx. 8.39 MM. Also included are three bezel mandrels, round, oval, and square, they are made of hardened tool steel with a smooth finish and even taper. The round bezel mandrel is approximately 1/4 - 5/8" (approximately 6.35-15.88 mm). The oval bezel mandrel is approximately 5x6 - 11x12.5 mm. The square is approximately 5x5 - 8x8 mm. The first hardwood bench pin measures 6 x 2 x 5” (15.3 x 5 x 12.7 cm) and is cut with a V-slot. The second hardwood bench pin measures 7 1/4 x 2 x 1 and has a round and an oblong cut out in addition to two v-slots and square and half round sawing support. This makes it ideal for uses like ring sizing, piercing and stone setting. Both of the bracelet mandrels are hardened steel. The oval measures 6” (15.3 cm) long plus the tang. The round one is also 6" (15.3 cm). Both measure approximately 7 inches by the tang and 4 inches by the tip. The bench pin and the bracelet mandrels fit perfectly into the opening under the anvil (not at the same time) and are held securely when the whole device is clamped to a table with the screw mechanism.
